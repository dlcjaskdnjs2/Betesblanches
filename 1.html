Betes blanches

Betes blanches means white beasts. There is only the will to do our job.

We are not constrained by time and money. only do what we want Even if it looks stupid, I think it is sincerity for beauty.

Competition ruins art. We don't compete. There is nothing perfect about being beautiful. Therefore, we only create our own beauty without any hindrance.
